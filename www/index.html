Smart Routing, Zero Config

Decentralized intelligence for your .NET 9 microservices.

ConduitNet eliminates the need for complex gateway configurations. Instead of hardcoding rules, your gateway learns the topology in real-time. If a node isn't the leader, it transparently redirects traffic to the correct peer, keeping your infrastructure simple and your application smart.

Built for speed using System.IO.Pipelines and MessagePack, it bridges the gap between raw gRPC and heavy frameworks like Orleans. You get the performance of machine-to-machine RPC with the architectural simplicity of standard services. No sidecars, no proto files, just code.
